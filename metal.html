Julien Laguerre

Perhaps I lost momentum, being so obsessed with the UI over the past few weeks. And the other day when I saw Openai's Sora, I told my friend "it kind of defeats the point of doing what I'm working on." Now that I swallowed it, my ideas are clearer. No, not at all.

What I do is semantics, understanding. It reaches from a declared 3D scene all the way down to actual grounded understanding of a situation. And that is something.

The Semantify3D project is about about making meaning - not colours, or sounds, but meaning itself - live and deployed in a 3D space, in time.

I'm now using Wails, from Lea Anthony. It basically offers a fast & clean Golang backend, behind a familiar and comfortably beautiful HTML/CSS/JS frontend. I'm working on a side-project that's ideal for getting acquainted with that tool, a 3D version of Nine Men's Morris. I'll post a link if I finish it.
